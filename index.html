About Us

Tropical Beauty Spa & Salon

Tropical Beauty Spa & Salon is a full-service beauty destination in the heart of Pompano Beach, offering everything from hair styling and coloring to skincare treatments, manicures, pedicures, and body care.

Our experienced team is dedicated to making every guest feel pampered and refreshed. Whether you need a quick blowout, a full color transformation, or a relaxing spa day, we have you covered.
